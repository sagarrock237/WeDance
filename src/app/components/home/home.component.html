About Us!

Raj Bhardwaj is the visionary founder of WEDDANCE, a premier wedding choreography company renowned for transforming ordinary celebrations into extraordinary experiences. His innovative approach combines traditional and contemporary styles, ensuring that every performance is not only stunning but also deeply personal. Whether it’s a first dance, a family flash mob, or a group performance, Raj Bhardwaj and his team at WEDDANCE are here to make your wedding dreams come alive on the dance floor.
